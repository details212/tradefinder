What is TradeFinder?

TradeFinder is a desktop app for active traders. It brings together trade ideas, charts, and market context so you can spot opportunities and review setups in one workspace. Sign in with your account to sync preferences and use the full app.

  • Curated trade ideas and tools tuned for real-time market work.
  • Charts and analysis to dig into symbols and patterns in one place.
  • Secure sign-in; your session stays on your machine under your control.
